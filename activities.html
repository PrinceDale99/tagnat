A C T I V I T I E S

Teacher's Month Celebration

We celebrate the teacher's month inorder to thank our teachers and give them the rest they deserve. Alongside the the teacher's event student clubs also set up stalls in the back court where students can engage in some activities and buy some food the students of Home economics strand made.

Science Month Celebration

The students set up stalls which can be enjoyed during the students break time. It was also celebrated during the teacher's month which made it more enjoyable.

National Statistics Month

In order to celebrate the National Statistics Month, the students from Eureka : Science & Mathematics Club hosted a booth events for students to enjoy.

Talent Auditions

Other than celebrating events, Our school also offers Dance, Singing and Lip-Sync audition so that the students can showcase their skills and talents.

Sport Tryouts

Our school doesn't only focus on cultivalating artistic talents but also atlethic prowess. Our sport clubs conducted a Badminton, Basketball , Hip-hop/Aerogymnastic and Cultural Dances, and lastly taguig national Highschool Pep Squad tryouts, so that students can show off their flexible, athletic skills and be the part of TNHS sports representative.
